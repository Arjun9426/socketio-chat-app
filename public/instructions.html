hello this is a instructions page

  • Please give a username before starting, receivers will get message in form of "sender's_username: actual_message"
  • chat page consists of two columns, one for received messages, one for sent messages
  • if messages are sent without using "@receiver_username:" , then they will be broadcasted to all online users and will be logged as "broadcasted: actual message" in sent messages column, else it will be sent only to "receiver_username" and will be logged as "To receiver_username: actual_message" in sent messages column
  • messages "Broadcasted" by you are received by all members on site if they are online
  • if members are not online(browsers not having a chat tab), they will not receive message(no sync), else they will receive them under received messages column
  • message of this form "@username:actual_message" is received only by user with username as "username" in form like "sender's_username: actual_message"
